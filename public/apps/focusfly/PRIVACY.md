# Privacy Policy for FocusFly

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Introduction

FocusFly ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

## Data Collection

**FocusFly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.**

We do not:
- Collect any personal information
- Store user data on external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Collect location data (except for map display purposes, which is processed locally)
- Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

## Local Data Storage

All data created within FocusFly, including:
- Focus session history
- Flight routes and destinations
- App preferences and settings
- Any other user-generated content

is stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Permissions

FocusFly may request certain permissions for core functionality:
- **Location Services**: Used only for displaying your location on the map interface. Location data is processed locally and never transmitted or stored externally.

## Third-Party Services

FocusFly does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

FocusFly does not knowingly collect any information from children. Our app is suitable for users of all ages.
